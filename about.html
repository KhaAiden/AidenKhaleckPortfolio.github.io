Aiden Khaleck is a New Media Art student at San Jose State University, focused on the intersection of technology and nature. He works with multiple technological mediums, including 3D map projection and experimental video, to craft immersive experiences that highlight the delicate balance between these two realms. Through his work, he aims to illuminate the coexistence and mutual enrichment of technology and nature, provoking thought and inspiring dialogue about our relationship with the natural world in an increasingly digital age.
